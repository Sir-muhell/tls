Why Attend?

As the global economy continues to wrestle with the daunting realities of a world marked by extreme Volatility, Uncertainty, Complexity and Ambiguity (VUCA), it is more salient than ever for professionals to rethink the effect on their careers and adopt strategies to future-proof themselves.

Hence, becoming a Thought Leader – a reputable expert that people look up to for valuable insights and new ideas in the industry – is a strategic way to raise your game and create new opportunities for yourself.

    Leveraging Thought Leadership and Personal Branding, you can:

  • Distinguish yourself in a crowded industry of professionals as the go-to expert in your niche
  • Generate public attention which comes with economic benefits in form of paid speaking engagements, guest appearances at events, etc.
  • Impact people beyond your workplace and build a legacy
  • Be awarded and rewarded for your invaluable contributions to the advancement of your profession
  • Position yourself to attract high profile jobs and get paid premium
  • Take control of your time and how you work

At this edition of the Thought Leadership Series specially organised for HR Professionals, you will join 100+ who are gaining practical insights on how to achieve thought leadership in your industry and reap its benefits.
